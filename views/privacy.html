Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy. EcoForecast does not require user accounts for general use, and we do not collect personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or phone number unless you explicitly contact us.

2. Location Data

If you click on our interactive maps, we temporarily process those map coordinates (Latitude and Longitude) to query our weather APIs. This location data is processed in real-time and is not permanently tied to you or your IP address.

3. Cookies and Admin Sessions

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. We only use essential, secure session cookies strictly for the Administrators of the platform to access the backend dashboard securely.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to others. Coordinate data is passed securely to our partner APIs (Open-Meteo) strictly for the purpose of retrieving weather data.
