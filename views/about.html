About EcoForecast

Malaysian Weather Intelligence

EcoForecast is an advanced, AI-driven weather prediction and environmental analysis platform designed specifically for Malaysia. Our goal is to provide accurate, hyper-local insights into weather patterns, air quality (AQI), and environmental impact.

How Our AI Works

Our platform is powered by an LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) Neural Network. Unlike standard weather apps that just relay current data, our AI analyzes the last 15 days of historical weather data (temperature, humidity, pressure, and wind) to recognize complex patterns and predict future conditions.

Data Sources

We believe in transparent, open-source data. All of our raw meteorological data is generously provided by Open-Meteo. Our interactive mapping system utilizes OpenStreetMap.
